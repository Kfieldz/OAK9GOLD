The Sengoku period in Japan was from 1467 to 1615 and was by far one the bloodiest times in Japanese history, with a very popular Demon King by the name of Oda Nobunaga. With the death of the Ashikaga Shogunate the realms of Japan were throwm into an upheaval of struggles and hard times, all of those with power trying to establish themselves as the ruling Emperor of Japan. The "Warring States" period lasted until the creation of the Tokugawa shogunate in 1615. There were several persons trying to claim Japan, others to unify it. One man by the name of Toyotomi Hideyoshi
